Myself

  to go out with my friends and have a blast,
  to have money left over to help the less fortunate,

Data Scientist

  I hope to pursue this career because I feel that
  it is a job that challenges me almost all the time,   requiring skills that I am not very good at,
  and it meets my needs for the career that
  I want to pursue.

Cafe

  the fragrant smell of pastries in the air
  when I no longer have my main job,
  and my parents and aunt will be shareholders
  of this shop, because then we can talk and be closer.
